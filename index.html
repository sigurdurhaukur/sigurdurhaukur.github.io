So when did this happen this fear of the police in communities of color?

In the US police were created in the 1700 hundreds to track runaway slaves. That was the beginning of a system that has tormented African American communities for decades. Then the slaves were freed 1863 and the 13th amendment ensured that. Although this may have seemed like a good idea, slave owners in the south needed another source of free labor. Slaves did most if not all of the heavy labor back then and did it for free. So there were no slaves due to the civil war and huge gap in labor that needed to be filled. Slavery was abolished be the 13th amendment, but there was a loophole. It read that it would abolish slavery and unvoluntary servitude, ecxept as punishment for a crime. So all throughout the nineteenth century the police were called on black people over nothing. Millions went from slave to prison worker. An agenda was pushed that African Americans were more dangerous and needed harsher treatment. When Black people wanted to move to white neighborhoods and integrate, the police were almost always called. Rights that they should have had were stripped away such as economic, political and social freedoms. These ways of expressions were punished and people of color were jailed for the smallest things.

This has been happening for years and it got even worse after president Nixon war on drugs. In the 1970s there were two things that concerned the American government : hippies and black people. That was said by one of Nixon's top advisors, he then added :"You understand what I'm saying? We knew we couldn't make it illegal to be either against the war or black, but by getting the public to associate the hippies with marijuana and blacks with heroin. And then criminalizing both heavily, we could disrupt those communities, we could arrest their leaders, raid their homes, break up their meetings, and vilify them night after night on the evening news. Did we know we were lying about the drugs? Of course we did." At the time Nixon was against two things the anti war hippies and the African Americans who were demanding their human rights. Drugs were heavily criminalized during this time and he enforced that the new sentencing would be two to ten years for possession of marijana, this was of course to jail the hippies.
